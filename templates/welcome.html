While we not so patiently await the arrival of the new baby, we thought we could have a bit of fun with the waiting and wondering game.

There are currently 7 categories you can bet on

  • Baby's Birth Date
  • Baby's Birth Time
  • Baby's Birth Weight
  • Baby's Birth Length
  • Baby's Birth Hair Color
  • Baby's First Name Initial
  • Baby's Middle Name Initial

Once you have placed your bets, venmo @Manda-Phadke $2 per category and you will offically be entered to win the pot for that bet! 100% of the pot will go to the winner of the bet.

Winners will be chose based on the baby's birth characteristics, if no one has chosen the exact characteristic the closest guess will be selected, if there is a tie, the one that is under will be the winner.
